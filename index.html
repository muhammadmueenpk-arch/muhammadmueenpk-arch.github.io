I began my career on-site, installing fiber optic and structured cabling systems, and over the past 5+ years, I have built hands-on experience delivering telecom and low-voltage infrastructure projects across Pakistan and Saudi Arabia.

I specialize in low-voltage systems, including Building Management Systems (BMS), Fire Alarm, Access Control, and CCTV installations. My expertise spans the full project lifecycle — cable installation, device termination, testing, and supporting system commissioning — ensuring every installation meets approved design standards and quality benchmarks.

Committed to continuous growth, I am currently pursuing my CCNA certification to deepen my knowledge of network infrastructure and IT systems. My goal is to build on this strong field foundation, take on larger and more technically complex projects, and evolve into a well-rounded professional bridging low-voltage systems with modern network engineering.
